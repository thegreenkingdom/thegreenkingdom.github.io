Introduction

“The Green Kingdom”, is a living dream of a wise man, also a famous, sculptor, artist and creative enthusiast Mr.R.Ramdev. In his 150 Acres of vast land, tastefully being designed and developed, with trees, Plants and sculptures from around the world. It is located at near HITEC corridor, Hyderbad which is a fast growing and developing side of the city.

The project is aimed at spellbinding people from all around the world to experience the magic of heterogeneity residing within creatively designed built environments and unique concepts.

About the Challenge!

Cottage Design Challenge 2021

The cottage can serve a small family, or a couple or a group of friends. You can design or set the surrounding context of your cottage to favour your design using natural or manmade elements.
Take the Challenge to Brush up your creativity in designing the cottages and find an inviting design that will deliver the cozy, comfortable and relaxed living you thought only existed in fairy tales. Living Unique, And Living It All!.
